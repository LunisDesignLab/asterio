# Deals — pipeline

A **deal** is created when a broker and an investor have agreed to proceed with a purchase
(they are past discussion, moving toward acquisition). The "My deals" module tracks these.

## Stages (off-plan transaction milestones, in order)

1. **EOI** — Expression of Interest submitted.
2. **Down payment** — initial payment made.
3. **SPA signed** — Sale & Purchase Agreement signed. **Point of no return — 100% locked in.**
4. **Investor signed**
5. **Developer signed**
6. **Submitted to DLD** — submitted to the Dubai Land Department.
7. **Oqood signed** — off-plan registration (Oqood) completed with DLD.

## Notes

- Stages are sequential; **SPA signed** is the commitment threshold (deal is effectively
  closed/binding from here).
- A deal links one broker ↔ one investor ↔ one listing/unit.
